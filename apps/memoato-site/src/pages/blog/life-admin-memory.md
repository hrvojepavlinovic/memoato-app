Life admin disappears into tiny moments.

You buy a battery. You pay a fee. You fix something. You promise to send a document. You remember it today, but six months later you cannot find the details.

Memoato is meant for those small logs.

## The useful note is often simple

Examples:

- `Bought Toyota battery, 59 EUR, 2 year warranty`
- `Paid kindergarten trip 18 EUR`
- `Called dentist, next appointment in July`
- `Promised to send invoice tomorrow`

That is not a habit. It is not a calendar event. It is not worth a full document.

But it is worth remembering.

## Raw first, then structure

Memoato stores the raw sentence first.

Then it can extract useful pieces:

- Item or service
- Person or organization
- Amount
- Warranty
- Date
- Reminder context

So later you can search:

- When did I buy that car battery?
- How much did I pay?
- What did I promise to send?
- When was the last dentist appointment?

## The missing layer between apps

Todo apps are good for tasks.

Calendars are good for scheduled events.

Email is good for communication.

Memoato is for the small context that does not cleanly belong anywhere else, but becomes valuable later.
